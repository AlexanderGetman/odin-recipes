Takoyaki

Japanese street food classic!

Takoyaki, also know simply is octopus balls originated in Osaka in the first half of XX century. Now it's one of the most popular street foods in Japan!

Ingredients

  • Eggs & flour
  • Dashi-flavored batter
  • Octopus
  • Beni shoga (pickled red ginger)
  • Green onion, Tenkasu, Bonito flakes
  • Kewpie mayo, Takoyaki Sauce & Garnish

Steps

  1. Mix the batter.
  2. Prep all of the fillings.
  3. Oil the pan, pour the batter, add in the fillings.
  4. Flip takoyakis 90 degrees, add some batter. If done correctly, you'll have nice round takoyakis, crispy all around.
  5. Put 'em on the plate, brush with souce, add mayo and sprinkle bonito and aonori.
